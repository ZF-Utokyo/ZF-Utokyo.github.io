AI X Finance

Hi, I am Fan Zhang.

I am a second-year PhD student at The University of Tokyo, supervised by Prof. Yu Chen. In 2025, I joined MBZUAI as a visiting student in NLP, where I worked with Prof. Preslav Nakov and Dr. Zhuohan Xie on financial LLM evaluation, financial reasoning benchmarks, and AI agents for financial analysis. Before that, I was also a visiting student at NUS AIDF, hosted by Prof. Kewei Huang.

Open to collaborations and opportunities from both academia and industry.
